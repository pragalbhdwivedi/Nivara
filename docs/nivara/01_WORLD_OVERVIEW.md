# World Overview

## World Name: Nivara
A continent where seasons are not weather—they are politics.

## Setting Snapshot
Nivara is shaped by oaths, matriarchal power structures, and a living winter that answers conflict with balance. Its defining tensions revolve around who can claim legitimacy, who can bind power, and who can end wars.
