# Maalkor

## Role
Maalkor leads the Ash Dominion, an infection of frenzy rather than a nation. He is the force that turns grief into riots and makes peace feel humiliating.

## Domain
- **Ash Oaths**: Rage, crowds, forgetting.

## Leadership and Methods
- Rules alongside the Ash-Brides, war-oracles crowned in soot and gold.
- Can erase names from memory, creating “ghost wars.”
- Frames the Melt as liberation: “The world is tired of silence.”
