# Symbols and Sigils

## Faction Symbols
- **White Court (Frost)**: Crescent moon + crossed blades + snowgem.
- **Iron Sanctum (Law)**: A key inside a fortress.
- **Blood Choir (Prophecy)**: Red thread woven into a crown.
- **Ash Dominion (Frenzy)**: Black spiral ember.

## Pranish Sigil / Emblem Design

### Core Concept
A symbol that communicates silence, winter, and decisive justice.

### Final Sigil (Primary Version)
- **Shape**: A pointed teardrop shield, like an icicle tip.
- **Central motif**: Two crossed swords (one long, one short) forming an X.
- **Snow-gem**: A small six-point star snowflake at the crossing point.
- **Crown element**: A thin crescent moon above the blades, facing upward like a bowl.
- **Silence mark**: A single vertical line down the shield’s center behind the swords.

### Heraldry Palette
- **Primary**: Ice Silver, White, Deep Midnight Blue.
- **Accent**: Glacial Cyan gemstone at the center.
- **Royal variant**: Thin gold outline on the shield edge.

### Variants
- **Minimal mark**: X swords + center snow-gem inside a circle (tattoo/ring/stamp).
- **War banner version**: Shield + swords + moon with two trailing torn ribbons.
- **Wax seal**: Circle border text: “PRANISH — WHITE SILENCE” (or “प्राणिश — श्वेत मौन”).

### One-Line Lore
“Where steel crosses beneath the moon, winter decides.”
